About Us

StartupsJo "Your Gateway to the Jordanian Entrepreneurship Ecosystem" is Jordans’ destination for startups, investors, and support ecosystems. Supported by The Innovative Startups and SMEs Fund (ISSF) and managed by the Information and Communications Technology Association (int@j),

StartupsJo objective is to connect entrepreneurs, investors, and other ecosystem players in Jordan with each other while providing digital tools and training on starting and doing business to improve the “Investment Readiness” of entrepreneurs and ultimately, increase deal flow.
